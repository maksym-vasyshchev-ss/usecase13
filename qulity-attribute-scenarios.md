# Quality Attribute Scenarios

This section lists and prioritizes the scenarios:

N. | Quality Attribute | Scenario | Business priority
---|---|---|---
QA-1 | Security | At all times the credentials and data should be transferred over encrypted channel | High
QA-2 | Performance | Under normal conditions, tax recalculation should take no more than 5 seconds | High
QA-3 | Reliability | Under normal conditions, no data loss is acceptable | High
QA-4 | Availability | Under normal conditions, system should operate with 95% SLA | Medium
